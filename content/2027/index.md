OSCW 2027 will take place coincidentally with [Real World Crypto](https://rwc.iacr.org/2027/) on a to-be-announced date in 2027 in Seattle, WA, USA.

## Call for Proposals

We are requesting proposals for in-person sessions, both presentations and hands-on/interactive demonstrations, on topics of interest to those who work on and with open source cryptography.

Sessions are offered in three lengths: **20 minutes** (with 5 minutes Q&A), **35 minutes** (with 5 minutes Q&A), and **50 minutes** (with 5 minutes Q&A), with an additional 5-minute break between sessions. We especially encourage interactive and workshop-style sessions.

We plan to record sessions and release recordings under the [CC BY-NC-ND 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/4.0/) license, subject to speaker consent.
